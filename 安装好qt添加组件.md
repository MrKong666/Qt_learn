![输入图片说明](https://github.com/MrKong666/Qt_learn/blob/main/img/%202024-10-05%20200252.png?raw=true)

![输入图片说明](https://github.com/MrKong666/Qt_learn/blob/main/img/%202024-10-05%20200327.png?raw=true)
